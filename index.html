    My Motive

    My Motive is to help project owners, by offering a wide range of services to facilitate improvement in alignment, productivity, organizational effectiveness, teambuilding and team relations to ensure superior project performance​. We aim to improve the client business performance through enabling execution excellence and effective project delivery and help business owners implement a solid execution plan and a robust contracting strategy for their projects

    My Mission

    My mission as a Executive Coach/ Spiritual Coach is to empower and inspire my clients to live a life that is authentic, fulfilling, and aligned with their core values. I believe that everyone has the potential to create positive change in their lives, and I am here to support you on your journey towards personal and professional growth and transformation.

    My Approach

    My approach to coaching is rooted in positive psychology, mindfulness, and cognitive-behavioral therapy, reinforced by the great insights from Indian Intellectual Tradition. I work collaboratively with my clients to help them identify their strengths, overcome limiting beliefs, and develop practical strategies for achieving their goals. I believe that coaching is a process of self-discovery, and I am committed to providing a supportive and non-judgmental space for my clients to explore their inner world.

    My Services

    I offer a range of coaching services, including one-on-one coaching sessions, team coaching, alignment workshops, and online courses. My areas of expertise include executive coaching, project leadership coaching, business leadership coaching, and personal development.
